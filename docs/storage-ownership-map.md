## Repository-Backed Stores

### postgres_tracked_positions

- Role: Production tracked-position rows, reviews, closes, proof/readback counts, and runtime health.
- Location: `DATABASE_URL`
- Route contracts: `tracked_positions_close, tracked_positions_create, tracked_positions_read, tracked_positions_review`
- Tables: `position_reviews, repository_schema_migrations, tracked_positions`
- Migrations: `tracked_positions_postgres_0001_current_schema_baseline`
- Constraint groups: `api_service_enforced, db_enforced, deferred, proof_contract_owned`
- Index groups: `candidate_deferred, db_existing`

Hard rules:
- Tracked positions are Postgres-owned in the browser product.
- Missing or failing DATABASE_URL fails closed through the unavailable sentinel.
- Do not silently fall back to SQLite.

- Role: Local paper and hypothetical suggested-trade workflow state.

- Route contracts: `suggested_trades_close, suggested_trades_create, suggested_trades_read, suggested_trades_review`
- Tables: `repository_schema_migrations, suggested_trade_reviews, suggested_trades`
- Migrations: `suggested_trades_sqlite_0001_current_schema_baseline`
- Constraint groups: `api_service_enforced, db_enforced`
- Index groups: `candidate_deferred, db_existing`

Hard rules:
- Suggested trades remain separate from tracked-position proof rows.
- Suggested trades are not broker fills and do not feed production proof truth.
